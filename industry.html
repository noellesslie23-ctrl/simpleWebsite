    • Cybersecurity Issues

  • Cybersecurity issues that could arise in the healthcare industry are ransomware and data breaches. In ransomware attacks, the attackers will encrypt important systems and it will stop operations then they will demand that the hospital pays them to stop it. In data breahes, the attackers will steal data from hospitals as it is very sensitive and important information.

    • Cybersecurity Best Practices

  • A few cybersecurity best practices in the healthcare industry are protecting and encrypting data and regularly updating software. It is important that hospitals encrypt and protect their data so that patients personal, sensitive information is not easily leaked to the public. It is also very important that they regularly update their software as it also helps with security, but also it helps to keep the devices up to date and functioning.
